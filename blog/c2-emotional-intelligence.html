Blog#5 Emotional Intelligence

What learnings did you take from the chapter?

A lot of the learnings in chapter one reinforced a lot of what I know and have experienced about emotional intelligence. To be honest as I was reading through the chapter I was reflecting more on my current state of EQ, how strong my EQ was and why certain positive habits are no longer a part of my life. In reading this chapter and reflecting over my past year (which was a real year of change for me), rather figuratively beat myself for not maintaining things it’s helped me become more understanding and kind towards myself. I never had a daily practice of mindfulness and I believe that is the lesson for me. Build one. This is where I am and with time and mindfulness practice – I can rebuild the level EQ I had, surpass it and develop it into my way of being.

How was my first practice of two minutes of mindfulness?

I went into my two minutes open to moving between the Easy way and Easier way – which ever felt right during the session. I started in easier way and to be honest with the noises and happenings in my house at that time I kept finding my focus distracted. So I switched to Easy Way and focused on my breath and I did feel moments of purely being and then ‘bing’ – my two minutes were up. I really enjoyed it and will make that my next tiny habit to incorporate next week – two minutes of mindfulness before I go to bed.
